About Me

Hello, I am John Gavin, founder of The Kandy Kitchen, Seattle's Sri Lankan Restaurant. My passion for cooking came from childhood when I spent plenty of time in the kitchen with my mother and aunt. I was fascinated by the process, all the unique spices and ingredients thrown into the pot

to make something magical. Whenever I visited Sri Lanka, I was always eager to try all the exotic-sounding spices and dishes I had never heard of before. In school, I developed an interest in business and economics, and later in life, I was lucky enough to find some friends who shared my passion for business

and cooking. Together, we founded the restaurant to share authentic Sri Lankan cuisine with Seattle. Your wonderful experience here is the product of countless weeks of research and practice honing in our craft to bring out the essence of what makes Sri Lankan Cuisine so Immaculately Mind-bending
